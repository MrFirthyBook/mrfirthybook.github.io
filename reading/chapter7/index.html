			Reading List: Chapter 7: Mental Health

For easy access, here are the links to additional resources discussed in Chapter seven of 'Practical Web Accessibility'. These links will provide supplementary information and learning from what's covered in the chapter, allowing you to deepen your understanding in this area.

Reading List

Just delete me

A library that provides tips on how to delete accounts on popular sites more easily. It also rates sites on how easy it is to delete your account, ranging from easy to impossible.

Deceptive Patterns by Harry Brignull

Harry Brignull's website dedicated to 'Deceptive Design' (previously dark patterns). The site explains a range of patterns, examples of them on the web, and which laws they violate.

Dark patterns "hall of shame"

A user-submitted gallery of good / bad examples of dark patterns on the web. You can filter by company.

Sir Tim Berners-Lee - Contract for the Web

Created by the inventor of the Internet on the 30th anniversary of its creation. It lays out principles for governments, companies, and citizens to make the web a better place for everyone.

Tech Policy Design Lab - Dark Patterns

An initiative from the World Wide Web Foundation, the design lab is exploring ways to curb widespread practices of dark patterns, and move the web towards more trusted design.
