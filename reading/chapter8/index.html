			Reading List: Chapter 8: Imagery

For easy access, here are the links to additional resources discussed in Chapter eight of 'Practical Web Accessibility'. These links will provide supplementary information and learning from what's covered in the chapter, allowing you to deepen your understanding in this area.

Reading List

Practical example

This chapter's practical example includes how to make an accessible background video, an example of the <picture> tag responding to a dark mode preference, and examples of various icon implementations over the years.

Design accessible animation — A11y project

A series of links maintained by the A11y Project about how to design and build animations in an accessible way.

FontAwesome icon library

The most popular icon font in the world. If you're looking to use standard icons in an accessible way with ease, you should check it out.

Smashing Magazine's write-up about SVG a11y

An overview of every possible combination of attributes you can add to SVG's in order to make them more accessible, and which ones are the most robust.

Screen reader tests on SVG a11y combos

Smashing Magazine's breakdown of how every combination of attribute on SVG's are read out by a range of different screen readers.
